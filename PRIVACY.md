# Privacy Policy — Golden Ratio Overlay

**Effective: July 12, 2026**

Golden Ratio Overlay does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

- **No data collection** — the app has no analytics, no telemetry, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs.
- **No network access** — the app never connects to the internet.
- **No accounts** — there is nothing to sign up for or log in to.
- **Local settings only** — your preferences (selected guide, color, overlay position and orientation) are stored locally on your Mac in the standard user defaults of the sandboxed app container, and never leave your device.

Because the app processes no personal data, there is nothing to delete, export, or opt out of.
